Why Arbor Lake

Specialized Care in Farmerville

Arbor Lake Skilled Nursing & Rehabilitation has served North Louisiana for over a decade — offering a homelike atmosphere, an experienced clinical team, and the full spectrum of post-hospital care.

🏥

Skilled Nursing & Rehabilitation

Our SNF wing features all private rooms with 24/7 skilled nursing — combined with on-site physical, occupational, and speech therapy.

🏠

Long-Term Care

A true home for residents who need ongoing care, with both private and semi-private rooms available.

🌳

D'Arbonne Cove Memory Care

A specialized, secured neighborhood for residents with Alzheimer's and dementia — trained in dignified, individualized care.

🧘

Hospice Support

End-of-life care delivered with compassion, in coordination with the family's chosen hospice provider.
